Director's Message

Dear Students! Your are starting an exciting journey of academic excellence and personal growth at one of Sindh's premier institutions of higher education. I know this voyage shared with so many others will be filled with personal rewards and mutual satisfaction!. The University is fully geared to help you shape your future and turn your dreams into reality. The curricula of all degree programs offered in 57 disciplines besides Pharmacy and Law (5-yr)- is wide spectrum to choose from, to suit your potential and genius. All this aims at providing new avenues for you to carve out your future. The University of Sindh is country's oldest institute of higher learning and also an internationally recognized research led comprehen-sive University. While recognizing the strength of its heritage and traditions, Sindh University also engages in frontier research and academic endeavors that reflect and address the needs of a fast changing, knowledge-based world. Teaching at Sindh University is closely integrated with research; our University's mission is to discover, preserve, and disseminate knowledge; produce creative work; and promote a culture of broad inquiry throughout and beyond Sindh. We aim to be the place of higher learning for talented men and women in Pakistan and particularly in Sindh, who would serve community, society and the nation. The University's transformative education and innovative research are aimed at making a distinct and positive impact on society. We hope you will be inspired by the many fascinating facets that make University of Sindh a leading Pakistani University in Sindh; your grooming in its peaceful academic environment will influence the future. The University is committed to advancing your academic growth and helping you through in carving out successful career. Together, we can stride confidently into the future, with University of Sindh making ever-greater contributions in education, research, leadership and public engagement, locally and globally.

Prof. Dr. Saima Qayoom Memon Director of Research & Graduate Studies
